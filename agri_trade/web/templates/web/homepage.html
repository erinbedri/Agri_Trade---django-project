Welcome to AGRI TRADE!

Our Solution

Agri Trade is a digital B2B marketplace that brings together Sellers and Buyers of agricultural products.

Agri Trade is not a broker - we do not buy or sell agricultural products, we merely offer Sellers to market their products easily on the market.

How It Works

  1. Register to the Platform
  2. Place your product to the Marketplace as a Seller by providing Buyers with:
    • crop characteristics and details
    • sell details
    • additional details
  3. Get contacted by potential Buyers with our integrated messaging system
